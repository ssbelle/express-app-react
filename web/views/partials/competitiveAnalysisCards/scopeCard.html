What is included

All the software solutions helping companies manage their finance and accounting functions will be covered in the feed. This will include all the legacy software companies be it on-premise liscenced or cloud-based software.

What is not included

Pure play survices firm that offers accounting related services. Companies focused only on financing will not be covered. Companies providing solutions for anyspecific sector will not be covered.
